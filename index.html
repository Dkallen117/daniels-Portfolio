About Me

My name is Daniel Allen. I am a Game Designer with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Game Design from Full Sail University. For as long as I can remember, creativity has been a driving force behind nearly everything I do. From winning third place at the World Competition for Odyssey of the Mind in Boulder, Colorado (as a 5th grader), to successfully coordinating a band to create a 6-song EP and perform at venues across Northeast Georgia, my creative passion never ceases to display itself. This passion also transfers over to my love for video games and my desire to create them. From blocking out and designing intricate levels, to writing captivating stories, and then testing them all (QA testing), I've dabbled in most aspects of Game Design and will continue to broaden my horizon. My personal favorite video game is Halo, and once I laid my eyes upon that game (also as a 5th grader), I knew instantly that I would help to continue that series, or help create other games that allows the players to be absorbed into what they’re experiencing as they play, as if they’re a part of the universe I helped create.
